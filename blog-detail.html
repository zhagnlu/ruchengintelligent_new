About Rucheng Technology

Rucheng Technology is a professional manufacturer specializing in CNC Press Brake, CNC Shearing Machine, Metal Cutting Machines, and Sheet Metal Processing Equipment.

Our products are widely used in industries such as automotive manufacturing, telecommunications, and computer hardware.

Advanced Production Capabilities

We possess advanced production lines and first-class testing equipment, adhering to international quality management systems to meet global standards.

Strong R&D and Innovation

Innovation is at the core of our business. We have a dedicated R&D team that continuously develops and upgrades our CNC Press Brake, CNC Shearing Machine, and Metal Cutting Machines. Our engineers work closely with production teams to turn new ideas into reliable technologies.

Commitment to Excellence

Our mission is to build a first-class brand in the field of CNC sheet metal machinery. We aim to become a globally recognized leader by maintaining focus on technology leadership, process control, and customer service.

Why Choose Rucheng?

  • Over 20 years of experience in CNC press brake manufacturing
  • Wide product range: from Mechanical Blade to Robotic Equipment
  • Exported to over 50 countries worldwide
  • Custom solutions for your unique metal forming needs
